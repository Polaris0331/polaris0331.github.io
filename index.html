Meat, eggs, and dairy products might be produced without animal slaughtering.
肉蛋奶制品的生产，可以不用屠宰动物。

You may wonder, how could we produce them without animals? Are they a kind of “frakenfood” pieced together from all sorts of materials by human? Are they edible?
你可能会想，没有动物，如何生产肉蛋奶呢？是用各种材料拼凑而成的“怪胎食品”吗？这种食品可食用吗？

The answer lies in cellular agriculture.
答案就是细胞农业。

What exactly is cellular agriculture and how are cultured versions of traditionally animal-based food produced?
细胞农业究竟是什么？细胞农业如何另辟蹊径，培养传统的动物性食品？

Cellular agriculture is the production of animal-based products from cell cultures rather than directly from animals. After hunting and domesticating animals, cellular agriculture looks set to become the third phase of human sourcing of animal protein.
细胞农业，通过细胞培养生产动物产品，而非直接从动物身上获得。在狩猎和畜牧之后，细胞农业似乎将带领人类进入获取动物蛋白的第三个阶段。
